This program was designed to enable ESL students to encorporate correct pronunciation of difficult English sounds into their motor (muscle) memory.

Since pronunciation is only an issue for advance ESL students, the lesson is given entirely in English. However, a brief description is given below in Japanese.

How To Practice English Pronunciation

English pronunciation training is similar to playing a wind instrument such as a flute.

We exhale and vocalize while holding a certain face position, then we change face positions to the following sound.
